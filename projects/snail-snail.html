Kevin Chen

Snail Snail

Summer 2022

Snail Snail is a 16 page four-color risograph zine about snails. Created in collaboration with Ishana Shastri.

Limited initial release (summer 2022) of 50 copies, second edition (spring 2023) of 30 copies.

I designed, color-separated, and printed. Illustration split between the two of us.

This was my first ever risograph project, created a week after I had learned how to use the machine.
