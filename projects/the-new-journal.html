The New Journal, Volume 55

Fall 2022 –

I am currently the Creative Director of The New Journal, a student magazine that publishes creative non-fiction about Yale and New Haven. The New Journal was awarded “Best Student Magazine in the Country” by the Society of Professional Journalists in 2021.

Over the last three issues, I revived and redesigned the original serif wordmark, redesigned the entire editorial design system, and designed most spreads. Currently two more issues (for a total of five) left of volume 55.
