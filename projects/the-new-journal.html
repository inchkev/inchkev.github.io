The New Journal, Volume 55

Fall 2022 –

Currently the Creative Director of The New Journal, a student magazine that publishes creative non-fiction about Yale and New Haven. It was named the “Best Student Magazine in the Country” by the Society of Professional Journalists in 2021.

Over the last three issues, I revived and redesiged the original serif wordmark, redesigned the entire editorial design system, and designed most spreads. I coordinate layout, illustration, and photography direction and manage a team of 5 designers. Currently one more issue (for a total of five) left of volume 55.
